# kanban-board-evidence-sync

## Goal
Sync kanban board status with card statuses and add evidence links for completed slices.

## Why
Stale board signals reduce coordination quality and slow verification.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Board entries reflect current card status.
- Slice-complete cards include evidence links (acceptance index).
